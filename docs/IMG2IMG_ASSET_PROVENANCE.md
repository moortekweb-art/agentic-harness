OpenAI image-generation tool. They are decorative product assets, never trusted
status evidence and never runtime-generated content.

restrained teal, paper-and-anodized-metal materials, centered compact geometry,
and no text, people, code, logos, watermarks, neon, or busy backgrounds.
Follow-on images use the connection image as the mandatory style reference.

Runtime WebP files are resized to a maximum 720px edge and encoded at quality
82. The unmodified PNG outputs remain under `docs/assets/img2img/`. Functional
icons are hand-normalized SVG paths and are not derived raster icons.
